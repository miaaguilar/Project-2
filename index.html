What is the Amazon?

The Amazon Rainforest is a tropical rainforest covering the Amazon River and parts of South America. The Amazon is home to millions of animals, from insects, birds, mammals and more. As well as the fauna, the Amazon has a wonderful display of flora from tons of species of plants and many types of trees.
