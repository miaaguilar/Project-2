What's the problem?


Despite the fact that Amazon houses multiple species and provides 20% of the worlds oxygen, many people aren't taking the fall of the Amazon seriously.

The Amazon has around Native American 400 tribes. These tribes are affected every time there is a disaster. Deforestation and wildfires cause distress to those that live there, however, the Amazon doesn't get as much help as other places.

Notre Dame Cathedral had a fire and raised over 1 billion dollars, but the Amazon doesn't get that kind of money. Why is that? I'll leave you with that question.
