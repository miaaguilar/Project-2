The Science behind it all

Below are some scentific facts that go hand-in-hand with the Amazon Rainforest:

  • The Amazon provides a home to 10% of the known species on Earth.
  • The Amazon hols over 1500 bird species.
  • The toucan, a bird the inhabits the Amazon can be heard from over half a mile away.
  • Thanks to the major fight against deforestation, the rates of trees being cut down has decreased sligthly. This is still a very big issue.
  • The Amazon is known as the "Lungs of the Planet", it provides more than 20% of the world's oxygen.
