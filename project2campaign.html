Problems


The Amazon Rainforest is dying. Years of deforestation and wildfires are slowly breaking down the tropical rainforest. Loggers have been cutting down trees to be able to make room for their cattle, they use the Amazon's land for their own benefit. As well the cattle industry, many agricultural companies clear the land to be bale to grow their crops. Mining operations and paper operations use the Amazon for their own benefit.

Solutions

Some ways to help the Amazon, is to make sure that you donate and support organizations that are fighting to save the Amazon. Proper log cutting is also a way to support the Amazon. That means to ensure that the trees you are cutting down do not affect ecosystems and do not harm those that live in the area.

Take Action!

To be able to take some action, bring awareness to Amazon Rainforest, by posting on any social media. You can also donate to organizations such as Rainforest Alliance , WWF and Rainforest Foundation US.
